Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

GGFit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the GGFit mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password. Your password is securely handled by Firebase Authentication and is never stored in plain text.

Profile Information

You may optionally provide personal details such as age, height, weight, gender, fitness goals, and experience level. This information is used solely to personalize your experience and provide tailored recommendations.

Fitness & Health Data

The app stores data you enter, including:

  • Workout history (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Meal and nutrition logs (calories, macros)
  • Supplement tracking (supplements taken, schedules)
  • Personal records (exercise PRs)
  • Custom workout routines
  • Body weight tracking
  • Diet goals and preferences

Device Information

We may collect device-level information such as device type and operating system version for the purpose of delivering push notifications and ensuring app compatibility.

Push Notification Tokens

If you enable reminders, we store your device's push notification token (via Expo Push Notifications) solely to deliver the reminders you have opted into. This token is not used for advertising or shared with any third parties.

Local Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device (such as active workout session progress, app preferences, and theme settings) using on-device storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains entirely on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Personalize your workout and nutrition recommendations
  • Track your fitness progress over time
  • Send supplement and workout reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve the app experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication). All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and you continue to use the app. After you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Google Firebase — for secure data storage and authentication

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app
  • Update or correct your profile information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings section of the app. When you delete your account, all of your personal data, workout history, meal logs, and other stored information will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

8. Third-Party Content

The app may link to third-party content, such as exercise demonstration videos hosted on YouTube. These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites or services.

9. Children's Privacy

GGFit is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

10. International Users & Your Legal Rights

If you are a resident of the European Union/European Economic Area, you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Please contact us to exercise any of these rights.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
